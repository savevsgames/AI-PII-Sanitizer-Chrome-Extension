## 2. Description of Service

Prompt Blocker is a browser extension that protects your personally identifiable information (PII) when using AI chat services by:

- Substituting your real PII with aliases in requests sent to AI services
- Converting aliases back to real information in AI responses
- Encrypting and storing your identity profiles locally on your device
- Providing optional cloud backup features (premium feature)
- Monitoring and logging substitution activity for debugging purposes

**Supported AI Services:**
- ChatGPT (OpenAI)
- Claude (Anthropic)
- Gemini (Google)
- Perplexity AI
- Microsoft Copilot
- Poe
- You.com
