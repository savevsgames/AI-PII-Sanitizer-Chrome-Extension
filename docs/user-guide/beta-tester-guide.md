## ğŸ› Known Issues & Workarounds

### Issue: "Extension context invalidated" error
**What it means:** You updated the extension while the page was open
**Fix:** Hard refresh the AI chat page (Ctrl+Shift+R)

### Issue: Protection not working on a page
**What to do:**
1. Check the extension badge - is it green?
2. Hard refresh the page (Ctrl+Shift+R)
3. If still not working, reload the extension

### Issue: Alias not being replaced in responses
**What to check:**
1. Is your profile **active** (toggle is ON)?
2. Did you spell your name exactly as in your profile?
3. Try refreshing the AI chat page

## ğŸ”’ Privacy & Security

### What Data is Collected?
- Your alias profiles (encrypted and stored in Firebase)
- Usage statistics (how many replacements, which services)
- NO actual message content
- NO browsing history

### What Data is Shared?
- Nothing! Your data is private and encrypted
- Only you can access your alias profiles
- Google authentication is for syncing only

### Can I Delete My Data?
Yes! At any time:
1. Open PromptBlocker popup
2. Go to Settings tab
3. Click "Delete All Data"
4. Your aliases and account will be permanently deleted
